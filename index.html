Early-career Network for AI, Culture, Technology & Society

Exploring how AI shapes — and is shaped by — society, politics, and culture

The global rise of artificial intelligence has brought about a range of complex questions about the way in which AI increasingly shapes, and is shaped by, society, politics, and culture. ENACTS brings together a community of like-minded early-career researchers across the social sciences, humanities and computer science who work in this interdisciplinary field — and who should probably meet.
